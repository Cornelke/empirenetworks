our process

Our process is very simple from contact discovery to project launch. This process is eesential to prevent mishaps during project development. This includes the steps below:

Contact

Once the client makes contact through any of our chanells, we make up a follow up call inorder to understand the client needs and requirements after which we make a proposal after careful consideration for the client to review and make changes.

Contracting

After the proposal phase, the company follows procedure and designs a contract for the project. This contract protects both parties from any problems that may occur and the ways forward.Once in agreement sign the contract, the project moves forward.

Development

Development follows according to contract and within the time allowed. The payment is done according to contract and the client is informed of every milestone completed. Once complete its tested and changes are made based on the contract.

Launch/Delivery

After completion is done delivery made to the client after complete payment based on the contract. After that the project is considered complete for both parties and everyone is satisfied. Refferals may be made for future projects.
